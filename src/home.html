About

Our Store

Better Together Coffee is a cozy café located in Kugenuma Kaigan, Japan, where we bring a taste of Australian coffee culture to our coastal town. Founded by an Aussie at heart who spent years in Australia, we're dedicated to serving great coffee made from the finest beans. We're all about connecting people over quality brews and warm conversations. Come in and experience a little slice of Australia, right here in Japan!

Our Coffee

At Better Together Coffee, we’re passionate about sourcing only the finest beans for our brews. We carefully select our beans from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring they’re ethically grown and harvested. Each batch is roasted to perfection, bringing out the unique flavors that make every cup special. Whether it’s a rich, bold espresso or a smooth, balanced pour-over, our dedication to quality shines through in every sip.
